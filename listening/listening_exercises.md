# Listening Exercises & Prompts

1) Dictation drills (短文): 20 sentences from NHK Easy; dictate and compare.
2) Question prediction: before audio, list 3 possible question types and 2 keywords to listen for.
3) Shadowing sets: 10 dialogues, 3x shadow each.
4) Speed scaling: play at 0.75x → 1.0x → 1.15x for each clip.
5) Error tag: vocabulary, particle, inference, time-sequence. Log under `planning/four_week_tracker.md`.
