# Daily Study Schedule (Sep 30 – Dec 7, 2025)

Use this calendar alongside `planning/four_week_tracker.md` and your error log. Each day lists primary actions; adjust time of day (morning/afternoon/evening) to fit your routine. When a task references ranges, work from the annotated CSVs/TSVs generated in the repo (e.g., `vocab/core_words_001-150_annotated.csv` or `tools/vocab_reading_core_words_001-150.tsv`).

Legend:
- **Vocab** — new items + SRS reviews using reading→kanji decks (`tools/quiz.py` or Anki).
- **Grammar** — patterns from `grammar/grammar_100.csv` and drills in `grammar/exercises_01.md`.
- **Reading** — passages in `reading/` or external articles (NHK/Asahi). Always time yourself and summarize aloud.
- **Listening** — sources from `listening/resources.md`; shadow whenever possible.
- **Mock/Notes** — larger timed sets or admin tasks.
